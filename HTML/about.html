About Qaram

Qaram has been operational since 1 July 2014 as a closed corporation, B-BBEE Level 4 compliant. We specialize in air-conditioning, refrigeration, electrical, plumbing, maintenance, and gas installations, delivering superior service across Mpumalanga, Limpopo, Mozambique, and Swaziland.

Our Mission

To deliver superior service with an unmatched client experience, resulting in the highest levels of respect from our community and a rewarding environment for our employees. We never accept past performance as good enough and continuously strive for new levels of excellence.

  • Exceed client expectations through service, communication, and quality workmanship.
  • Produce quality workmanship through attention to detail and innovative solutions.
  • Offer the highest quality equipment to uphold manufacturer warranties and support clients.
  • Treat clients and team members with utmost respect and maintain a positive work environment.
  • Support and encourage a safe and enjoyable workplace for everyone.
  • Maintain a neat and tidy appearance while respecting clients and company property.

Our Vision

We aim to create value and demonstrate the vast potential of our company. Our goal is to provide clients with ultimate comfort and support. With Qaram, you can rest easy knowing we have your best interests in mind.

  • Deliver the best in equipment quality, technology, support, and customer experience.
  • Promote a culture of engaged employees who take ownership, show enthusiasm, and positively impact the company’s reputation.
  • Maintain innovation and never settle for the status quo.

Our Motto

"Look Beyond With a Breeze"

  • Anticipate and rethink the future without complications.
  • Move forward in an easy, effortless manner to open up prospects beyond our field of expertise.
